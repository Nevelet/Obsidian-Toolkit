## 📝 Appunti




**Ultime note create:**
```dataview
LIST
WHERE
	!contains(file.tags, "daily") and
	contains(file.outlinks, this.file.link) or
	contains(string(file.frontmatter), string(dateformat(this.file.day,"yyyy-MM-dd")))
SORT file.ctime asc
LIMIT 50
```
